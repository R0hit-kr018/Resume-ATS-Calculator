Frequently Asked Questions

What is an ATS and why does it matter?

ATS (Applicant Tracking System) is software used by 98% of Fortune 500 companies to filter resumes before human review. It scans for keywords, skills, and formatting. If your resume isn't ATS-optimized, it may never reach a recruiter.

How accurate is the ATS score?

Our algorithm simulates common ATS parsing logic using keyword extraction, skills matching, and format validation. While no tool can perfectly replicate every ATS (each vendor differs), our score correlates strongly with real-world ATS pass rates.

Is my resume data private?

Yes! All processing happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your resume and job description never leave your device or get sent to any server.

What file formats are supported?

We support PDF, DOC, DOCX, and TXT files up to 500KB. For best results, paste text directly as some PDF formatting may not extract perfectly.

How do I improve my score?

Focus on: 1) Adding missing high-priority keywords from the job description, 2) Including required skills in your skills section, 3) Using standard section headers (Experience, Education, Skills), 4) Avoiding graphics, tables, and columns that confuse ATS parsers.

Can I use this for multiple job applications?

Absolutely! Run a new analysis for each job application. Each job description has different keywords and requirements, so your score will vary.
